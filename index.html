About Envision

In the dynamic landscape of modern Bangladesh, where technology is rapidly reshaping the socio-economic fabric, Envision stands as a beacon of innovation and progress. Envision is a leading entity dedicated to catalyzing digital transformation in Bangladesh, working closely with the Access to Information (a2i) program to realize the vision of a Digital Bangladesh.

At Envision, our mission is to harness the power of technology to drive inclusive growth, empower citizens, and foster sustainable development across Bangladesh. We are committed to leveraging digital solutions to overcome societal challenges, enhance governance, and create a thriving ecosystem for innovation and entrepreneurship.

Central to our approach is our collaboration with the Access to Information (a2i) program, a groundbreaking initiative jointly implemented by the Government of Bangladesh and the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP).
